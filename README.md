### The minimalistic Neofetch alternative!

<p>
- Almost no Delay <br />
- Minimalistic <br />
- Reminds you on taco day <br />

How to configure Taco day? <br />
Change TACOTIME (line 11) to your week day. <br /> 
Default is 6 (Friday). <br />
